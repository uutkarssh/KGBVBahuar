Our Facilities

A safe, fully equipped environment designed to support every aspect of student life and learning.

Infrastructure & Amenities

What KGBV Bahuar Provides

Our campus is equipped with modern facilities to ensure a holistic residential education experience — from smart learning spaces to secure accommodation and healthcare support.

🏠

Hostel Facility

A clean, well-maintained residential hostel with a capacity of 100 students. Dormitories are spacious, well-ventilated, and monitored round-the-clock by dedicated housekeeping and supervisory staff to ensure safety and comfort.

📺

Smart Classrooms

Eight modern classrooms equipped with projectors, audio-visual aids, and educational software. The digital learning setup enhances comprehension and makes lessons more interactive and effective for all grade levels.

📚

Library

A well-stocked school library with NCERT textbooks, reference books, encyclopaedias, story books, and periodicals. Encourages a reading habit and serves as a quiet space for self-study and research outside class hours.

🔬

Science Laboratory

A functional science laboratory with equipment for Physics, Chemistry, and Biology practicals. Hands-on experiments make abstract concepts tangible and prepare students thoroughly for board-level practical examinations.

💻

Computer Lab

A dedicated computer lab with functional systems and internet connectivity. Students receive digital literacy training, basic programming concepts, and practical computer skills essential for higher education and employment.

⚽

Sports Ground

A spacious outdoor sports ground supporting games such as Kho-Kho, Kabaddi, volleyball, and athletics. Regular physical education sessions promote fitness, teamwork, discipline, and healthy competition among students.

🍽️

Dining Hall

A hygienic, spacious dining hall where balanced and nutritious meals are served three times a day. The menu follows government-prescribed dietary guidelines to ensure every student receives proper nourishment for growth and learning.

🏥

Health & Medical Room

A dedicated health room with basic medical facilities and a first-aid kit. Regular health check-ups are conducted in collaboration with local health authorities, ensuring early detection of health issues and prompt medical care.

💧

Clean Drinking Water

Purified drinking water is available throughout the campus via water purification units. Adequate sanitation and hygiene facilities, including separate washrooms for students, are maintained to the highest cleanliness standards.

🔒

Security & CCTV

The campus is secured with boundary walls, a monitored entry gate, and CCTV surveillance. A dedicated security guard is on duty at all times, ensuring a safe environment for all students residing in the hostel.

Built for Every Girl's Future

Our facilities are designed to support academic excellence, physical well-being, and personal growth — providing everything a student needs to thrive.
