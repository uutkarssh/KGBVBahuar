About the School

A School Built on Commitment to Every Girl

Kasturba Gandhi Balika Vidyalaya (KGBV) Bahuar is a Government residential school for girls from educationally backward sections of society, established under the Samagra Shiksha Abhiyan of the Government of India. Located in Jamalpur, Mirzapur, Uttar Pradesh, the school provides free residential education to girls from Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Other Backward Classes, and minority communities.

Our mission is to ensure that every girl in the region has access to quality education in a safe, nurturing environment — breaking barriers, building confidence, and securing futures. With trained teachers, modern facilities, and a supportive hostel, KGBV Bahuar stands as a pillar of girls' education in Mirzapur district.

What We Offer

School Highlights

KGBV Bahuar provides a holistic residential education environment designed to nurture academic excellence and all-round development.

🏠

Safe Hostel

Secure, comfortable residential accommodation with round-the-clock supervision ensuring the safety and well-being of every student.

📺

Smart Classrooms

Digitally equipped classrooms with projectors and audio-visual tools that make learning interactive and engaging for all students.

📚

Library

A well-stocked library with textbooks, reference materials, and recreational reading to foster a love for knowledge and literature.

⚽

Sports

Dedicated sports ground with facilities for outdoor games, promoting physical fitness, teamwork, and leadership among students.

💻

Computer Lab

A functional computer laboratory providing essential digital literacy skills to prepare students for the demands of modern education.

🍽️

Nutrition Program

Balanced, nutritious meals served daily in a clean dining hall, ensuring every student is healthy, energised, and ready to learn.
